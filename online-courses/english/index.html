Our online legal English programmes

We have two intensive online legal English programmes for you, which are the Illuminate programme, and the Activate programme. Both are based on the professional English for law courses at our prestigious school in Cambridge, England.

The Illuminate Legal English Programme

lluminate is a ten-course online legal English programme for international lawyers and law students. This practical and up-to-date training opportunity invites you to study the legal vocabulary and common law concepts needed to work in commercial law at an international level. A major aim of Illuminate is to increase your employability in an increasingly competitive job market. This course does what a legal English dictionary cannot help you with!

Illuminate is based on our legal English courses in Cambridge, where we have taught English to international commercial lawyers from all over the world for more than twenty years. The main focus of the programme is the vocabulary of commercial contracts, the vocabulary of companies and other forms of business, and the practical issues that any lawyer needs to consider in order to develop valuable commercial awareness.

Your current level of English does not matter because Illuminate allows you to work at your own speed. You can watch or read the study material and take the quizzes as many times as you need to, because each module is available for 40 days after purchase. All international lawyers and law students can benefit from Illuminate.

You can purchase individual courses according to your interest and you will receive an e-certificate of completion for each course. If you complete the entire programme of ten courses you can obtain the prestigious Illuminate completion certificate, which we will send to you from Cambridge, as a great addition to your CV.

There is no other legal English training course as beneficial as Illuminate on the market anywhere in the world. Start studying today and take a huge step towards your future in international commercial law!

The Activate Legal English Programme

The Activate online legal English programme is an effective and intensive online grammar course for legal professionals.

Employers of lawyers tell us that absolute accuracy in written and spoken English is just as important to a good lawyer as his or her knowledge of commercial legal vocabulary. Legal vocabulary does not exist in isolation but is surrounded by ordinary English words, often in very complex sentences. A bad preposition or verb tense can change the meaning of a legal document. Activate will help you to avoid that kind of expensive mistake.

This twelve-module programme has been designed with your accuracy in mind. When you draft or amend a contract, negotiate with commercial lawyers from other jurisdictions, or write a professional email in English, your grammar should be correct.

The programme works to activate your existing language knowledge and elevate it to the level required in a competitive legal job market. You can study as quickly or as slowly as you like, because this programme is available to you for 150 days after purchase.

Activate includes modules on all the verb tenses, modal verbs, conditionals and their variations, verb patterns, countable and uncountable nouns, prepositions, pronouns and determiners, and participle and relative clauses. It is highly interactive, and you can repeat all the content as many times as you like until you feel confident you can use the language appropriately.

Once you have completed the course, you will obtain an e-certificate as proof of your study and enhanced professional English language skills.

Start studying commercial legal English online today and give yourself a huge advantage in a very competitive job market!

Start your online course now!

The Initiate Legal English Programme

The Initiate Legal English Programme is an effective foundation-level online grammar course for legal professionals.

Grammar and the correct use of language are important to employers. Accuracy is just as important to a good lawyer as his or her knowledge of legal vocabulary. To be able to write professional emails in English, amend contracts drafted in English, or speak to an important client in an online call, a lawyer needs more than just legal vocabulary. Initiate is your first step towards avoiding expensive mistakes.

This twelve-module programme has been designed to support lawyers who feel they would benefit from beginning a careful study of grammar from the start. You can use this programme either to learn completely new information or to review your existing knowledge before you progress on to more difficult study material.

The programme teaches you some essential vocabulary, followed by grammar. The first two modules teach you some essential legal vocabulary and useful phrases. Then you will go on to study essential grammar points while also reviewing the legal vocabulary from the first two modules.

Initiate includes modules on verb tenses (present simple, present continuous, past simple, past continuous), ways of expressing the future (will and going to), the passive, modal verbs in the present tense, first and second conditionals, countable and uncountable nouns, key phrasal verbs, articles, and prepositions. It is highly interactive, and you can repeat all the content as many times as you like until you feel confident using the new language.

Once you have completed the course you will obtain an e-certificate as proof of your study and enhanced professional English language skills.
