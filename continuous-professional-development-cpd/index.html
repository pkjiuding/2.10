持续专业发展 (CPD)

我们所有的在线课程都获得了 CPD (CLE) 认证，当您完成整个 Illuminate 课程时，我们可以向您颁发 10 小时 CPD 的 CPD 证书。 我们还可以为我们的其他课程 Activate 和 Initiate 提供 10 小时的 CPD，这些课程也获得了 CPD (CLE) 认证。

Illuminate 在线计划

Illuminate 是学习有用的法律英语的一种新的有趣的方式。 该计划教授商业法律词汇和普通法的重要方面。 这些词汇都与书面商业合同、合同法、公司以及企业在财务上和侵权法下的责任性质有关。 该计划完全认可 CPD 积分，我们将授予您一份证书，在您完成该计划后，您将获得 10 小时的 CPD 时间。 随着您的进步，Illuminate Program 还会颁发TOLES官方培训-CAMBRIDGE LAW STUDIO剑桥法律英语证书！

Activate 在线计划

Activate 是专门为律师设计的语法复习课程。 该计划在法律背景下教授中级至高级语法。 Activate 旨在帮助您记住和修改您可能已经忘记的英语语法，并支持您学习可能对您来说陌生的语法结构。 如果您完成课程，我们将在您完成后奖励您 10 小时的 CPD 时间。 Activate Program 还可以在完成后授予TOLES官方培训-CAMBRIDGE LAW STUDIO剑桥法律英语证书！

启动在线计划

Initiate is a useful foundation-level legal vocabulary and grammar course. The programme teaches some introductory vocabulary and phrases, then goes on to teach and practice grammar in accurate legal contexts. This is the course for lawyers or law students who would like to begin at foundation level and build on their skills from there. If you complete the course, we will award you 10 hours of CPD time when you complete it. The Initiate Programme also awards a Cambridge Law Studio certificate upon completion!

Why take CPD accredited courses?​

Employees in many professions such as law, banking and accountancy take part in continuing professional development (CPD) every year.

For lawyers in some jurisdictions, the number of CPD points/hours required is decided by their legal regulator, which is often their Bar Association. Completing these points/hours may be a condition of remaining qualified to practise law. It is regarded as essential towards ‘continuing competence’.

CPD keeps your legal career moving forward and learning commercial legal English can be an important part of that. English is the international language of commercial law and having a good knowledge of the meaning of English contract vocabulary and the language of company law is vital.

Being able to write emails in error-free, accurate English to clients and colleagues is also a great skill to have. Also, a good appreciation of where the common law differs from a civil law jurisdiction can be a great asset to any lawyer, especially where the jurisdiction of a commercial contract dispute happens to be the UK, or the USA and the governing law is the English common law.

Even if you are a lawyer from a jurisdiction that has no formal requirement for CPD, you will still benefit enormously from CPD accredited courses. It will make you a more efficient employee and add to your value in the eyes of your employer.

We believe in the usefulness and quality of our legal English courses and we are delighted to be able to offer them to you, as a lawyer or an ambitious law student, as CPD accredited programmes.
